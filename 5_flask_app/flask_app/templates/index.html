About

Hi! I'm Richard Lu. I am a data scientist trained in the social sciences (PhD) and industrial and systems engineering (BS). My two interests of human behavior and optimization come together in helping people make better decisions, whether it is finding the most appropriate job or simply choosing where to eat. Given the inherent complexities of these areas, I believe the best approach is a mix of machine learning and causal inference methods in extracting the most information from limited data.

If you have an interesting problem you would like to chat about, feel free to contact me through the form at the bottom of my site.
